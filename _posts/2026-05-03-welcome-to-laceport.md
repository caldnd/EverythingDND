# Lace-Port

Welcome to Lace-Port, the Merchant Company’s headquarters.

Once a sleepy seaside port with a modest trade in textile exports, it has become ground zero for the Merchant Company’s expansion into Zaruthia.

The farmland surrounding the town has been bought, flattened, and replaced with vast warehouses. The local people who once lived here now toil as poorly paid workers for the Company.

Under an agreement with the Valenholt family—no doubt sealed with a considerable sum of gold—the Merchant Company now *is* the law. They collect taxes and dispense their own brand of justice under official warrant.

At the town gate, crow cages hang overhead. Inside them, rotting corpses sway in the sea wind. Nailed to the cages are their crimes:

- **Thief**
- **Traitor**
- **Debtor**

---

## Location

Lace-Port sits on the coast of **Bonebreaker Bay**.

The skyline is dominated by the imposing presence of **Fort Calico**—a massive coastal fortification bristling with cannons and weaponry.

---

## Districts

The town is divided into four main areas:

- **The Fort** — Military stronghold and seat of power  
- **The Merchant Town** — Administrative and commercial center  
- **The Docks** — Trade, shipping, and labor  
- **The Beach** — A harsh, windswept coastline  

---

## The Coral Isle

At the edge of the bay, where the sea begins to roll in earnest, a small island rises from the water—large enough for three ships to dock.

At its center, a towering oval formation of coral reaches skyward like a jagged crown.

The waters surrounding the isle are constantly patrolled by fast-moving boats.

No one approaches unchallenged.
